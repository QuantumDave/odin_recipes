Omlet

Description

This simple omelet recipe is quick, fluffy, and perfect for breakfast or a light meal. It’s made with just a few ingredients and can be customized with your favorite fillings like cheese, vegetables, or herbs.

The omelet cooks in minutes and delivers a soft, golden texture that’s both satisfying and easy to prepare even on busy mornings.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons milk or water
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon butter or oil
  • Optional: cheese, diced vegetables, or herbs

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl, add milk, salt, and pepper, then whisk until well combined.
  2. Heat butter or oil in a nonstick pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour in the egg mixture and swirl the pan to spread it evenly.
  4. When the edges start to set, gently lift them with a spatula, allowing uncooked egg to flow underneath.
  5. Add any fillings, fold the omelet in half, and cook for another minute until set.
  6. Slide onto a plate and serve warm.
