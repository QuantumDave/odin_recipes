Bacon

Description

This bacon recipe gives you perfectly crispy, golden-brown strips every time. Whether you like them slightly chewy or extra crisp, this method makes it easy to get consistent results with minimal mess.

Ideal for breakfast, sandwiches, or as a topping for salads and burgers, freshly cooked bacon adds flavor and crunch that instantly upgrades any meal.

Ingredients

  • 6 slices of bacon
  • Optional: paper towels for draining

Steps

  1. Place the bacon strips in a cold skillet, making sure they donâ€™t overlap.
  2. Turn the heat to medium and cook slowly, allowing the fat to render out.
  3. Flip the strips occasionally until they reach your desired level of crispiness.
  4. Once done, transfer the bacon to a plate lined with paper towels to absorb excess grease.
  5. Let it cool slightly before serving or using in other dishes.
